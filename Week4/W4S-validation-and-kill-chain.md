# W4S — Validation & Control-Plane Kill Chain

## Objective
Validate detection coverage against a realistic Azure control-plane attack sequence.

## Control-Plane Kill Chain
1. Initial access
2. Privilege escalation
3. Perimeter weakening
4. Credential access
5. Persistence or destruction

## Coverage Validation
Each detection maps to one or more kill-chain stages,
ensuring layered visibility across attacker progression.

## Gaps Identified
- Identity sign-in anomaly detection
- Conditional Access abuse

## Forward Look
These gaps inform Week 5 detection design priorities.

## Outcome
Demonstrates coherent, threat-driven detection architecture.
