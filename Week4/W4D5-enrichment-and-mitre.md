# W4D5 — Alert Enrichment & MITRE ATT&CK Mapping

## Objective
Enhance detections with contextual information to reduce analyst workload
and accelerate response.

## Enrichment Strategy
- Actor identity and role
- Source IP and geolocation
- Resource criticality
- Historical activity context

## MITRE ATT&CK Alignment
- Privilege Escalation
- Defense Evasion
- Credential Access

## Analyst Value
- Faster triage
- Higher confidence decisions
- Reduced false positive handling

## Outcome
Transforms raw telemetry into investigation-ready alerts.
